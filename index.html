Biography

I am currently a junior student at Tongji University, majoring in Computer Science and Technology.

Personally, I am a pragmatist and believe that all the advancements in CS are only meaningful when it is applied to the real world. Because of that, even though I am broadly interested in the methodologies of AI, my biggest interest lies in the technologies that have clear applications.

Currently, I am at Intelligent Big Data Visualization Lab (iDVx) as an intern. I am also actively reaching out to other fields both inside and outside CS to try and observe more aspects of AI.
